About Tim

With more than 12 years of broadcast experience at the high school, collegiate and semi-professional levels, Tim Spoerl has provided a constant blend of excitement, knowledge, and professionalism to the Kansas City area.

Tim currently serves as the play-by-play voice for Sports Radio 810 WHB's Game of the Week in Kansas City; a position he has held since 2013. While with 810, Tim has broadcasted two NAIA men’s basketball national championship tournaments, multiple Kansas and Missouri high school state championship events, and hosted weekly pre and postgame shows.

For the last three years, Tim has also served as a play-by-play broadcaster and program host in the local market for semi-professional and collegiate sports through KCMG Broadcasting Group.

Prior to his time with KCMG, Tim was the voice of Avila University from 2015-2019. While at Avila, Tim provided play-by-play commentary for all football, men’s and women’s basketball and baseball games. Tim also covered multiple NAIA national tournament events through his work with Avila.

Ever versatile, Tim has recently broken into lacrosse taking over play-by-play duties at Rockhurst University for the men’s program in the spring of 2021.

A native of Tulsa, Oklahoma, Tim graduated from Benedictine College with a degree in journalism and mass communications and currently resides in Kansas City, Missouri.
